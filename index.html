About

About Hin Saeng Nakorn Company .

  • 1996-2005

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Hin Saeng Nakorn Co., Ltd. is a Thai company involved in import and export of natural stone. The company has been active under its current name since 1996. The company is managed by a foreign director & sales manager who together have a natural stone expertise that has been built up over decades.

  • After 2005

    An Agency is Born

    Previously Hin Saeng Nakorn concentrated on exporting materials from Asia to Europe, Australia and the United States, since 2005 the company altered its focus and has begun concentrating on the local Thai market.

  • December 2012

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our activities in Thailand have taught us that Thai consumers are very interested in high quality foreign materials. Foreign imported materials currently available in Thailand have the stigma of being too expensive and is therefore often overlooked by Architects and Interior Designers.

  • July 2014

    Phase Two Expansion

    This perception is untrue and we are the proof. It is our goal is to provide high quality, new, unique and exciting natural stone products at a price that everybody can afford.
